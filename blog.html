September 2023 · IIT University
IEEE Extreme

The IEEEXtreme is a 24-hour global programming competition hosted by IEEE, designed to challenge university students around the world with complex algorithmic problems. During this event, participants work in teams under the mentorship of an IEEE member, competing against thousands of others across time zones and institutions. The competition covers a broad range of coding topics, including algorithms, data structures, and real-world problem-solving scenarios. Open to IEEE student members, IEEEXtreme provides an intense yet rewarding experience, enabling participants to showcase their technical skills, collaborate effectively, and gain recognition in the IEEE community.

CODESPRINT Competition

CODESPRINT is an exciting annual programming competition that challenges university students to solve a series of complex coding problems within a limited timeframe. Hosted by educational institutions or tech organizations, CODESPRINT aims to foster coding skills and encourage innovative thinking among participants. Competitors, often grouped into teams, tackle real-world problem statements using advanced algorithms and efficient coding practices. This event not only serves as a platform for students to showcase their programming abilities but also offers networking opportunities with peers and industry professionals, making it a valuable experience for budding tech enthusiasts.

May 2024 · University of Colombo
National Olympiad in Informatics

The National Olympiad in Informatics (NOI) is a prestigious programming competition aimed at high school students interested in computer science and algorithms. Held annually, the NOI challenges students to solve complex coding problems that test their problem-solving and logical thinking skills. The event is part of a global initiative to inspire young minds in the field of informatics, with winners having the opportunity to represent their country in the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI). By participating in the NOI, students gain exposure to advanced programming techniques, preparing them for future academic and career pursuits in technology.
